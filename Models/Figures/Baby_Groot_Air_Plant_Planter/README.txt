# Summary

Baby Groot's head has been hollowed out so he can be used as a planter for *Tillandsia* sp. "air plants". Air plants are widely available at retail and via the internet. They're very hardy and fairly low-maintenance, and as their name suggests, they need no soil.

Thanks to the original artist Byambaa for sharing this wonderful sculpt.
