# Summary

Got tired of my hand cramping so i made a holding stick  to paint keycaps
has a hole in the bottom for a 10x2mm magnet 

Note: you cant change the scale because the cross will be too big/loose otherwise.

It is pretty easy to change in blender though

# Print Settings

Printer Brand: Creality
Printer: CR-10S
Rafts: Doesn't Matter
Supports: Yes
Resolution: .2
Infill: 10

Notes: 
the tip needs supports printing on its side so the layer lines are perpendicular to the force of putting on and removing keycaps.
